About Gusto Bites

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Gusto Bites started as a small neighbourhood kitchen with a big idea: showcase local produce and simple technique. We believe food connects people and drives community.

Our mission: serve honest food with transparent sourcing, respectful techniques, and approachable flavors.

Core Values
  • Local Sourcing:We partner with regional farms.
  • Sustainability:Minimal waste and seasonal menus.
  • Hospitality:Warm service and inclusive dining.
Timeline
  1. 2022-Grand opening
  2. 2023-Community pop-up events
  3. 2024-Expanded menu and private dining

Teacher Notes & Grading Checklist

Use this checklist when assessing student sub,issions (HTML-only)

  1. File is a single HTML file named index.html
  2. Uses se,antic elements: header, nav, main, section, article, footer
  3. Includes media elements: <img>, <video>, <audio>, <iframe>
  4. Forms have table and a logical structure
  5. Tables are used where appropraite and include captions/thead/tbody when needed
  6. interactive HTML5 elements used: <details> <Summary>, <meter>, <progress>
  7. Accessible Feature: alt attributes, time, address, and clear link text
  8. Content quality: meaningful descriptio, transcripts for media, and clear organization

Suggested rubic:

  • Functionality & media presence: 35%
  • Semantic structure & accesibility: 30%
  • Contenyt6 quality & Completness: 20%
  • Extra elements & Completness: 15%

Teacher hint: Ask students to provide a short README in a comment block at the top of their submission, describing replaced media files and an d devitions.
